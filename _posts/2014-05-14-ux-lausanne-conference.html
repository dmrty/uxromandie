UX Lausanne is a 2 days conference in Lausanne, Switzerland, on creating delightful User Experiences.

After 2 years of monthly UX Romandie meetups, UX Lausanne will take place for the first time in 2014. The 2 days’ format will bring together local & international UX professionals to share knowledge and gain expertise in the User Experience field. The conference will be held at Arsenic Theater, one of Lausanne's most vibrant cultural spot.

UX Lausanne is the first Western Switzerland event dedicated exclusively to User Experience Design. We look forward to connect you with some of the most influential UX practitioners, leading thinkers, talented developers and innovative designers.
