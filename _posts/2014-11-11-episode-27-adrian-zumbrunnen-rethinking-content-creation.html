Content creation is at the heart of the web. Yet the experience of writing content in today's CMSs disappoints (I'm looking at you Wordpress - in which I'm writing this very post). Adrian Zumbrunnen will come from Zürich to talk about the journey and learning of developing and designing better content creation interfaces for the web.

About Adrian Zumbrunnen

Adrian Zumbrunnen - former UX Lausanne speaker - is a User Experience Designer, Writer, Speaker and Coffee Enthusiast working in the beautiful city of Zurich. He creates memorable product and communication experiences by consequently putting the user in the center of all creative efforts. UX Lausanne audience really enjoyed his talk and we're super glad to have him again in Lausanne.
