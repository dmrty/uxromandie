Accelerating UX: A Design Process For The Incubator Era

Workshop information

Language: English

Duration: 2 hours

Participants: 40 max. (first-come, first-served)

Price: Free

Important notice: As we have a limited number of seats, no Techup or other form this time. Please use the Tito. People subscribing to Techup will not be registered.

Workshop description

Banks, insurers, big telecommunication companies and others from the old economy feel they are under the threat of the young and dynamic. Fail-fast is a system at the core of the lean startup concept designed to make you learn and grow at unbelievable speed. Allianz SE, the world’s largest insurance company, has created an incubator to build startups and to iterate fast following the lean UX concepts. How can you design better products and services in this new era?

In this hands-on workshop attendees will be presented to a design challenge to be solved through the design of a mobile app. The speaker will show details on how an incubator works and innovates, the templates they use at Allianz X and provide sources of inspiration.

About Alexis Brion

Alexis Brion is the Innovation Lead at Allianz X, the startup incubator by Allianz. There he scouts for business ideas and mentors a variety of projects related to the financial world. Alexis lectures design innovation at the University of Applied Sciences Munich, taking students through a practical course in which they work on modern design innovation processes.

As a UX designer Alexis also gained in the last years experience introducing design processes into companies and leading big redesign projects, working at small and big internet companies.
